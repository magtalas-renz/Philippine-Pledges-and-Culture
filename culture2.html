Festivals and Tradition

Festivals, or fiestas, are some of the most colorful displays of Filipino heritage. Celebrations like Sinulog in Cebu, Panagbenga in Baguio, Kadayawan in Davao, and Ati-Atihan in Aklan highlight the creativity, devotion, and solidarity of communities. Through vibrant dances, music, costumes, and rituals, these events bring people together in gratitude and joy.
